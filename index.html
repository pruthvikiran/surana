Way Wipers

Guide way wipers protect contact surface and guide ways from contamination and ensure fault-free operation. The Guide way wipers are specifically manufactured according to the shape and size of the guide.

Telescopic Covers

The Telescopic Covers protect machine tool bed/guide ways against damage by swarf and coolant.

Bellows

Bellows are used on all kinds of machines to provide protection for Guide ways and spindles, in those cases where no Telescopic Covers can be fitted due to space constrains

Rollaway Covers

Our Rollaway Covers are alternate to Bellow and Telescopic covers Covers where there is limitation of space as they consume very less space.
